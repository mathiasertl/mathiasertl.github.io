Hi, I'm Mathias Ertl.

Senior software engineer & team lead, based in Vienna, Austria.

I currently work at TTControl, where I lead an international team focusing on integrating AI into our development workflow, Linux application development, IT security and our CI/CD infrastructure.

As team lead I care about an open and friendly work atmosphere - I believe you can only perform to your full potential if you feel valued. I value pragmatism but also a commitment to quality; balancing the two yields good software with reasonable effort.

As a software engineer I specialize in Python and Django, cloud computing and backend systems, with deep knowledge of IT security and Linux systems.

When not working, I contribute to open source projects (most prominently django-ca). I'm a local activist and elected member of the district council, trying to make my local area a better place. I also enjoy gardening, cycling and reading.
